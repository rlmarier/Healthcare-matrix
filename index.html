Patient care — ACGME competency

The provision of compassionate, appropriate, and effective patient-centred care. Covers clinical assessment, diagnosis, treatment, and responding to individual patient needs across the care continuum. This competency is the most clinically proximal — it directly shapes what a patient receives at the point of care.
